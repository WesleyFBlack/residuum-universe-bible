## Purpose

This page is the public reference for the Residuum universe. It records the core books, major terms, structural ideas, and confirmed canon relationships across the series.

It is not a draft notebook, production file, or full private story bible. It is a stable reference for readers, reviewers, cataloging, and future continuity.

---

## Core Idea

The Residuum universe follows people caught inside systems that do not simply punish them. They measure, absorb, delay, classify, and outlast them.

The central question is not whether the system wins.

The question is what remains after the system has counted everything it can count.

---

## Core Works

### Reading Order

**Bramble Field → False Door → Stacked → Reckon → Limited Edition → Mirador**

**Locker 405** functions as the machine-side prelude to **Mirador**.

The series moves from mapping, to inheritance, to surveillance, to replacement, to memory, to doctrine.

---

### Bramble Field

**Function:** The analog origin of system mapping.

A triptych about classification, delay, and grief.

**Part I: The Board**

Human contact becomes chart. The chart becomes authority. Authority remains incomplete.

**Part II: Pending**

Insurance, trusts, police, debt, and workflow turn human need into procedural delay.

**Part III: The Phone**

A mother refuses false closure when the system asks for access to grief.

---

### False Door

**Function:** The system enters through biology, cognition, and permission.

A novel about inheritance, optimization, artificial warmth, and exits that are not really exits.

The system does not arrive as punishment. It arrives as help.

---

### Stacked

**Function:** The building becomes the system body.

A novel about access, surveillance, rooms, fobs, elevators, intimacy, and permission.

Space itself becomes the interface. The building can track movement, but not everything that matters.

---

### Reckon

**Function:** Human counting becomes evidence for post-human counting.

A census story about field labor, heat, false closure, compromised data, and the sale of confidence modeling.

Human error does not disappear. It becomes the argument for replacing human judgment with modeled confidence.

**Key line:**

Closed is a workflow status. Resolved is a confidence question.

---

### Limited Edition

**Function:** The human era becomes collectible.

A novel about scarcity, nostalgia, art, provenance, cultural memory, family care, and debt.

After human counting begins to give way to machine confidence, **Limited Edition** turns toward what remains: records, objects, songs, collections, memory, and the fragile value of things that cannot be fully optimized.

It asks what happens when unique human things become valuable because the world that made them is disappearing.

---

### Locker 405

**Function:** The machine-side origin layer.

A prelude about artificial warmth, stress regulation, baseline human response timing, and the machine label **PROJECT FREBT-5**.

It shows the cold origin before **Mirador** shows the human wound.

---

### Mirador

**Function:** The terminal human book.

A witness novel about fear, stillness, movement, doctrine, the Count, and the difference between watching and looking.

Micah brings back movement.

The village makes it count.

Mera leaves with the boy and preserves looking without law.

**Final logic:**

No ladder.

No door.

No count.

Only looking.

## Canon Terms

### Residuum

What remains after systems classify, consume, erase, or explain a life.

Residuum is not simply evidence. It is the leftover human material that refuses full absorption.

---

### FREBT

FREBT is the buried wound-word of the universe.

In the surviving Document, it appears only as:

**FREBT**

No hyphen.

No pronunciation guide.

No definition.

No formula on the page.

The term begins as a machine classification label and later becomes misunderstood, ritualized, and transformed by people who inherit fragments without understanding the original system.

---

### The Document

A single surviving system-log page.

It is not scripture.

It is not a manifesto.

It is not a sacred text.

It survives because the Grandfather kept it as evidence after fleeing the system.

## Important Canon Distinctions

### Looking vs. Watching

Looking is attention without possession.

Watching begins as vigilance and becomes control.

Mera looks.

The Grandfather looks.

The Watcher watches.

That difference is central to **Mirador**.

---

### Evidence vs. Scripture

The Grandfather did not preserve the Document as scripture. He kept it as evidence.

Later generations misunderstand what he knew.

That misunderstanding is part of the canon.

---

## Public Canon Rules

FREBT is always written as **FREBT**.

Do not write it as **FRET-DEBT** in-world.

Do not define it inside the surviving Document.

Do not treat the Document as religious scripture.

Do not make the Grandfather a prophet.

Do not make the Count mystical at its origin.

The system begins as machinery, procedure, classification, and fear.

The myth comes later.
